Google is a multinational technology company specializing in Internet-related services and products. Founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin, it is best known for its search engine, which is the most widely used in the world. Google also offers a wide range of products and services, including online advertising technologies, cloud computing, software, and hardware.

Instagram is a popular social media platform that allows users to share photos and videos, apply various filters, and interact through likes, comments, and direct messages. Launched in 2010, it has become a key platform for personal expression, influencer marketing, and brand promotion. Instagram is known for its visual-centric approach, with features like Stories, Reels, and IGTV enhancing user engagement.

 LinkedIn is a professional networking platform that allows users to create profiles showcasing their work experience, skills, and education. It facilitates career development and job searching by connecting individuals with employers, colleagues, and industry peers. LinkedIn also offers tools for businesses to promote their services, share content, and recruit talent.
